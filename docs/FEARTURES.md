# Product Pillars

AudiesChess is built around five core pillars.

1. Play
2. Learn
3. AI
4. Community
5. Profile

Every feature belongs to one pillar.

---

# PLAY

Purpose

Play chess in multiple ways.

Core Features

- Quick Match
- Play Online
- Play vs AI
- Play with Friend
- Custom Game
- Tournaments
- Daily Challenge
- Practice Positions
- Game History

Future

- Clubs Battle
- Team Match
- Arena
- Swiss Tournament

---
